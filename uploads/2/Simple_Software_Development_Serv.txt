This Software Development Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2026, by and between AlphaTech Solutions ("Client") and CodeCrafters Ltd. ("Contractor"). The Contractor agrees to develop a custom mobile application ("the Software") for the Client as detailed in Appendix A. The total fixed price for this project is $50,000 USD, payable in three installments: 20% upon signing this Agreement, 40% upon successful completion of the beta milestone, and the final 40% upon final acceptance by the Client. All development work is expected to be completed within a period not exceeding 90 days from the effective date.

The Client retains all Intellectual Property Rights to the Software, including source code and documentation, upon final payment. The Contractor guarantees the Software will be free of material defects for a warranty period of thirty (30) days following final acceptance. Any material changes to the scope of work described in Appendix A must be documented in a written Change Order, mutually signed by both parties, which may necessitate an adjustment to the timeline and total fee.

In the event of a material breach of this contract by either party, the non-breaching party may terminate this Agreement by providing fifteen (15) days' written notice. Upon termination, the Contractor shall return all Client property and submit invoices for all work completed up to the date of termination. This Agreement shall be governed and construed exclusively by the laws of the State of Texas. The parties agree that this constitutes the entire agreement between them, superseding all prior discussions.
